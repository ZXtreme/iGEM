An essential of product report: Funcdyes

PRODUCT POSITIONING

Funcdyes offers a business-to-business product. It serves as an optogenetic bioreactor, providing a functional and environment friendly dye with pretty color, faint scent, and antiseptic efficacy. The equipment and can be either applied in large-scale cloth dyeing industry, or used in an individual fabric workshop by the craftsman.

PRODUCT STYLE

Funcdyes is assembled with a transparent cylindroid equipment and a tube containing the engineered Pichia pastoris and its broth. The equipment consists of an airtight container for fermentation and the light control circuit placing inside and around the tank wall. It is also attached to the feed and refeed pipe, for inducer or ferment extract to flow.

PRODUCT USAGE

The leak proofness should be examined first. After checking and sterilizing, attach the tube with the feed pipe. The fermented broth will then be transported into the container. During the fermentation , all the pipeline should be tightly closed , and the blue light can be remotely controlled according to the actual requirement. The refeed pipeline cannot be opened unless the user needs to gather the sample. After crushing the cell and resolve the pigment, the cloth can be dyed either by soaking, printing, or steam dyeing.

SAFETY

Funcdyes guarantees its safety from two aspects.

For the process of producing dyes, P. pastoris is adopted as the engineered strain, which is less or no harmful to operators and environment. Besides, the equipment is easy to control, and requires no toxic raw material or violent condition.

For customers who use the product from Funcdyes, their safety is carefully considered and ensured. The component of dye, lycopene and patchouli, is harmless to human body, and they are extracted only when the cells are all crushed and dead.
